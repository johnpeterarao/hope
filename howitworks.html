How It Works

Friends of Hope, Inc. (HOPE) offers a fundraising platform that leverages the reach of your favorite celebrities and technology to raise funds for nation-building. The prizes offered to benefit public schools comprise of unique and exciting experiences donated by your favorite influencers. HOPE allows everybody (almost - see restrictions) to join and have an equal chance of winning. There are lots of laws in place to protect our donors and guarantee fairness. Please read through these before entering.

  • General Mechanics

    1. The duration for each campaign is listed on the campaign page.
    2. Each campaign features a once in a lifetime experience anybody has the chance to win. Well-known celebrities donate a unique experience to the public (you guys) through HOPE in order to raise funds to build public school classrooms. All proceeds from your donations go directly to charitable causes.
    3. This campaign is open to all legal residents of the Philippines
      • To keep things fair, full-time and part-time employees including consultants, interns, fellows and volunteers of HOPE, DepEd, and our celebrity partners, as well as members of their households and relatives up to the second-degree of consanguinity do not qualify for entry.
      • Any individual using a method that provides an unfair advantage over individuals who enter as prescribed by these official rules are subject to disqualification from all of HOPE's campaigns, subject to HOPE's discretion.
      • No individual may win more than one experience listed on our platform.
      • Campaign drawings are done in succession of each other. In the case that a single individual donor is drawn for multiple experiences, the individual will be awarded the prize of the first experience drawn and any succeeding prizes will be forfeited.
    4. To join the campaign, simply make a donation to HOPE. Visit [URL], choose your desired classroom-building experience and complete the registration form and payment. You get 1 campaign entry for every Php 20.00 you donate.
      • We use an automated random drawing software to ensure that all entries are considered equally. Each Php 20.00 donation is assigned 1 entry.
    5. When you enter a campaign, we'll collect some information about you. Check out our privacy policy [at URL/Below] to see what we collect and how we use it. By entering the campaign, you also agree to our privacy policy.
